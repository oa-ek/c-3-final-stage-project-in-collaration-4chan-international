# Creating a Utility Tool for the Dark Souls Series

### **Purpose of the Program**
FromSoftware created the world-renowned **Dark Souls trilogy** and its successor, **Elden Ring** (2022). These games are famous for their deep gameplay and the immense variety of character builds.

**The Problem:**
*   You start as a basic character (like a Knight) and upgrade stats (Strength, Health, Intelligence) as you progress.
*   The game offers vast equipment options, but **resources for upgrading gear and redistributing stats are limited**.
*   Experimenting in-game can be costly or impossible if you run out of rare items.

**The Solution:**
Our program allows you to **design and predict character stats without limitations** for all four games, saving you time and in-game resources.

---

### **How the Program Works**
1.  **Game Selection:** On the home screen, choose one of the 4 supported games.
2.  **Character Builder:** Enter any stats and select weapons, armor, or accessories.
3.  **Real-time Results:** Instantly see calculated data:
    *   **Damage Output:** How much pain you'll deal to enemies.
    *   **Defense & Resistances:** Your protection against physical attacks and magic.
    *   **Equipment Load:** Monitor your weight to avoid "fat-rolling."
4.  **Save System:** Save your build to reference it later while you play the actual game.

> [!NOTE]
> **Unique Design:** Each game's interface will match its specific aesthetic theme, weapon sets, and upgrade mechanics.

---

### **PS: Scaling System**
Weapons have a unique **scaling property** based on your character's stats (Strength, Agility, Intelligence). 
*   **The Formula:** Damage boosts are calculated using a specific formula tied to your stat levels.
*   **The Ranking:** This is shown via a letter grade system:
    *   **S:** Highest damage boost (Optimal)
    *   **A, B, C...**
    *   **D:** Lowest damage boost
